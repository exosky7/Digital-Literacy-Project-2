# Digital-Literacy-Project-2
This repository contains a comprehensive project focused on developing essential digital literacy skills for students. It covers key areas such as effective use of digital tools, safe internet practices, responsible social media usage, email etiquette, and awareness of cyber threats like phishing.

The project includes practical components such as structured reports, case studies, checklists, and real-world scenarios designed to help students understand both the benefits and risks of the digital world. It emphasizes the importance of online safety, professional communication, and responsible digital behavior, especially in an academic and professional context.

Additionally, the repository demonstrates the application of theoretical knowledge through assignments like creating infographics, writing formal emails, and analyzing cybercrime scenarios. These activities aim to build critical thinking and digital responsibility among learners.

This project serves as a useful resource for students looking to improve their digital awareness and preparedness for academic, professional, and online environments.
