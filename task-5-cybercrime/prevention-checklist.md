### **Prevention Tips**

*  Use strong, unique passwords for each account (avoid reuse)
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Never click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages
* Always verify URLs before entering login credentials (look for HTTPS)
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities
* Keep your apps, antivirus, and operating system updated

### **UPI & Financial Safety**

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be bank officials
* Double-check payment requests—receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN
* Verify the receiver’s name before sending money

### **If Something Goes Wrong**

*  Call **Helpline: 1930** immediately for financial fraud
* Report online at: **[https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)** (National Cyber Crime Portal)
