### **Do’s**

* Think before you post—consider how it may affect your reputation
* Keep your accounts private or manage privacy settings carefully
* Share accurate and verified information only
* Be respectful and positive in comments and interactions
* Use social media for learning, networking, and personal growth

---

### **Don’ts**

* Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, passwords, etc.)
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech
* Don’t believe or spread fake news without verification
* Don’t overshare emotional or impulsive posts you may regret later
* Don’t waste excessive time—avoid social media addiction
